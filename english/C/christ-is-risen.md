---
title: Christ Is Risen
---

---
<center>
<br/>
Christ is risen! Christ is risen!<br/>
He hath burst his bonds in twain;<br/>
Christ is risen! Christ is risen!<br/>
Alleluia! swell the strain!<br/>
For our gain he suffered loss<br/>
By divine decree;<br/>
He hath die upon the cross,<br/>
But our God is he.<br/>

</center>
