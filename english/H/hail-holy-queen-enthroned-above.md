---
title: Hail Holy Queen Enthroned Above
---

---
<center>
<br/>
Hail, Holy Queen enthroned above, O Maria.<br/>
Hail, Queen of mercy and of love, O Maria.<br/>
<br/>
Triumph, all ye cherubim, Sing with us, ye seraphim,<br/>
Heaven and earth resound the hymn:<br/>
Salve, salve, salve Regina! <br/>
<br/>
Our life, our sweetness, here below, O Maria!<br/>
Our hope in sorrow and in woe, O Maria!<br/>
<br/>
Triumph, all ye cherubim, Sing with us, ye seraphim,<br/>
Heaven and earth resound the hymn:<br/>
Salve, salve, salve Regina!<br/>
<br/>
To thee we cry, poor sons of Eve, O Maria!<br/>
To thee we sigh, we mourn, we grieve, O Maria!<br/>
<br/>
Triumph, all ye cherubim, Sing with us, ye seraphim,<br/>
Heaven and earth resound the hymn:<br/>
Salve, salve, salve Regina! <br/>
<br/>
Turn then most gracious Advocate, O Maria!<br/>
Toward us thine eyes compassionate, O Maria!<br/>
<br/>
Triumph, all ye cherubim, Sing with us, ye seraphim,<br/>
Heaven and earth resound the hymn:<br/>
Salve, salve, salve Regina!<br/>
<br/>
The cause of joy to men below, O Maria!<br/>
The spring through which all graces flow, O Maria!<br/>
<br/>
Angels, all your praises bring, Earth and heaven, with us sing,<br/>
All creation echoing:<br/>
Salve, salve, salve Regina! <br/>

</center>
